Admission

  The academic session of the school is from April to March.

  For admission into Class-I. Circular issued during November every year and Entrance test held 1st week of December. Admissions held strictly on merit basis.

  New Students are normally admitted in April every year subject to availability of seats. For class-II-X and XII.

  For admission into class-XI notification made during 1st week of June. Admission held strictly on merit basis.

  All necessary documents, progress report, transfer certificate from previous school, original birth certificate are to be submitted at the time of admission. The transfer certificate must be Counter-signed by the District Education Officer of the respective State Govt. or concerned board office.

  Admission will be granted only after a written test.

  Application for a transfer certificate should be made in prescribed form available at office.Application should be made in one month in advance in respect of withdrawal of the student. Otherwise, one month's fee will be charged in lieu of notice period. Transfer certificate will be issued only after all outstanding dues of the school are cleared.
